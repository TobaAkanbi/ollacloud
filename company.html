Our story

Infrastructure shouldn't have to live an ocean away.

For too long, the only "serious" way to run enterprise cloud workloads in Nigeria meant renting capacity in Frankfurt or Virginia.

That came with a quiet tax: latency on every request, foreign-exchange exposure on every invoice, support queues in distant time zones, and an uncomfortable answer whenever a regulator asked where your customers' data actually lives at night.

Ollasystems built OllaCloud to remove that tax entirely. We designed and deployed OCI-compatible rack-scale infrastructure - with over 2,000 CPU cores, up to 26 TB RAM, and up to 3.65 PB of storage entirely within Nigerian borders. Your data stays in Nigeria. Your invoices are in Naira. Your support team is in your timezone.

Today we operate from our Lagos availability zone on a 99.99% SLA, support fintechs, enterprises and public sector institutions, and we are building out the continent's most capable sovereign cloud platform.
