The platform

Nigerian Hosted Cloud platform - built for enterprise workloads.

One console, one Naira invoice, and a complete stack of OCI-compatible infrastructure primitives all running inside a world-class Nigerian data centre with zero foreign data exposure.

Compute

Shared or dedicated virtual machines with a high-resource footprint over 2,000 CPU cores and up to 26 TB RAM. Scale instantly without re-architecting.

Object & Block Storage

Up to 3.65 PB of combined balanced block, file and object storage S3-compatible, AES-256 encrypted at rest, replicated across zones for 11-nines durability.

Virtual Networking

Isolated Virtual Cloud Networks (VCNs) with subnets, route tables, gateways and an inbuilt three-layer firewall private by default, connected on your terms.

Managed Kubernetes

OCI-compatible managed Kubernetes clusters, control plane fully handled by OllaCloud. Deploy containers and workloads; we handle patching, scaling and availability.

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Automated backups and cross-zone replication with near-zero recovery time and point objectives. Business continuity built into the platform, not bolted on.

Managed Databases

Fully managed relational and in-memory databases with Exadata direct-connect support for lowest-latency app-to-database response times. Automated patching and read replicas included.

Data Residency Nigeria

Your data stays in Nigeria. Your costs stay predictable.

The business climate is dynamic, technology must adapt rapidly. OllaCloud delivers a privately hosted IaaS platform that is innovative, fluid and built for the speed of Nigerian enterprise.

  • Full data residency - NDPA compliant
    Every byte stored and processed within Nigerian borders. No foreign-jurisdiction exposure, ever.
  • Three-layer firewall & compute enclave isolation
    Your workloads run in a dedicated compute enclave - our admins have zero access to your infrastructure or data.
  • Pay-as-you-go in Naira - no Forex
    Pay only for the resources you use, settled in local currency. Eliminates the need to source foreign exchange.
  • Seamless migration from on-premise or public cloud
    Replicate your existing enterprise environment on OllaCloud with minimal or no modification required.
