Compute

High-resource compute built for Nigeria's most demanding workloads.

Built on OCI-compatible rack-scale infrastructure with over 2,000 CPU cores and up to 26 TB RAM. Choose shared or dedicated environments. Run traditional, cloud-native, big data and third-party applications without compromise.

  • Shared & dedicated infrastructure
    Subscribe to shared compute or provision a fully dedicated environment - 552 to 2,208 OCPUs with 6 to 26 TB RAM.
  • Hypervisor included at no extra cost
    Virtualisation is built in - no separate VMware licensing fees. Trusted partitioning saves on database licensing too.
  • Agile, rapid deployment
    Spin up instances and replicate your on-premise enterprise environment in minutes. Go to market faster without infrastructure delays.

Storage

Petabyte-scale storage encrypted, durable, Nigeria-resident.

Up to 3.65 PB of combined balanced block, file and object storage - plus up to 1.2 PB of high-performance block storage for your most demanding database and application workloads. All data encrypted at rest and replicated in-country.

  • S3-compatible object storage
    Point your existing tools, SDKs and migration scripts at OllaCloud - no rewrites required.
  • AES-256 encryption at rest
    All customer data is encrypted at rest. Our administrators have zero access to your data or infrastructure.
  • 1.2 PB high-performance block storage
    SSD-backed high-IOPS volumes for databases and workloads that demand consistent, low-latency throughput.

Networking

3-layer inbuilt firewall. Isolated by architecture, not just policy.

OllaCloud enforces a 3-layer inbuilt firewall and compute enclave isolation so every tenant's workload is separated at the hardware level. Carve out private Virtual Cloud Networks (VCNs), segment into subnets, and route traffic through managed gateways, all inside Nigerian borders.

  • 3-layer inbuilt firewall
    Network-level, host-level and compute-enclave isolation prevent any lateral movement between tenants.
  • Virtual Cloud Networks (VCN)
    Full control over IP ranges, subnets, route tables and internet or NAT gateways.
  • Managed load balancers
    Distribute traffic across instances with health checks and TLS termination - no external appliances needed.
Managed Kubernetes

OCI-compatible managed Kubernetes in Nigeria.

OllaCloud's managed Kubernetes service is OCI-compatible and upstream-compatible, your manifests, Helm charts and CI pipelines work without modification. We run, patch and secure the control plane while you focus on deploying and scaling applications.

  • OCI-compatible, upstream Kubernetes
    No proprietary lock-in. Standard kubectl, Helm, and CI/CD integrations work from day one.
  • Fully managed control plane
    Highly available masters, automatically patched and monitored - zero operational overhead for your team.
  • Autoscaling node pools
    Scale worker nodes to match demand and scale back to control cost - billed in Naira with no forex exposure.
Resilience & data

Two more services your business can lean on.

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Schedule automated backups of VMs, volumes and databases with policy-based retention, and replicate to a second availability zone. Achieve near-zero RPO and RTO restore point-in-time or fail over to a clean environment in minutes, not days.

  • Policy-based scheduled backups
  • Cross-zone replication & automated failover
  • Point-in-time restore with near-zero RPO/RTO

Managed Databases

Run production Oracle and open-source databases on OllaCloud's Exadata-direct-connect infrastructure for the lowest possible application-to-database latency in Nigeria. We handle provisioning, patching, backups and high-availability replication.

  • Exadata direct connect lowest app-to-DB latency
  • Automated patching, backups & HA replication
  • Read replicas & connection pooling built in
