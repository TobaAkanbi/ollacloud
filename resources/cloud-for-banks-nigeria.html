CBN compliance

What the CBN requires from your cloud infrastructure and how OllaCloud delivers it.

Data residency & localisation

The CBN requires customer data, transaction records, and payment data to be stored on Nigerian servers. CBN circular PSS/DIR/PUB/CIR/001/004 (June 2026) makes this a formal directive for all licensed institutions.

✓All data physically hosted within Nigeria
✓No automatic replication to overseas regions
✓Documented evidence of data location available

Third-party risk management

The CBN framework requires banks to conduct due diligence on cloud providers and maintain enforceable contractual controls. OllaCloud operates under Nigerian jurisdiction and provides the contractual framework required.

✓Nigerian-incorporated provider under CBN reach
✓Data Processing Agreement with localisation commitments
✓Audit-ready infrastructure documentation

Security & access controls

The CBN cybersecurity framework requires banks to implement technical controls against unauthorised access and to demonstrate those controls to examiners. OllaCloud's architecture provides these controls at the infrastructure level.

✓3-layer inbuilt firewall
✓Compute enclave isolation per workload
✓AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS in transit
✓Full access audit logging

Business continuity & recovery

The CBN framework sets expectations for RTO and RPO on critical banking systems. OllaCloud's infrastructure is designed around near-zero recovery objectives, with documented and testable recovery capabilities.

✓Near-zero RPO & RTO for critical workloads
✓Cross-zone replication & point-in-time restore
✓Recovery test evidence for examinations
