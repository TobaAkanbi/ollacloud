Built for Nigerian licensed operators

Every CBN-licensed fintech category. One compliant platform.

OllaCloud is designed for the operational and compliance profile of Nigerian fintech where regulatory obligations are non-negotiable and infrastructure downtime directly affects transaction throughput.

Payment companies & mobile money operators

CBN circular PSS/DIR/PUB/CIR/001/004 (June 2026) requires all payment operators to store and manage payment transaction data on Nigerian servers. OllaCloud satisfies this requirement by design your transaction data never leaves Nigeria.

Lending platforms & digital banks

Customer financial data: loan records, credit assessments, account histories is personal data under the NDPA. Hosting this data on overseas cloud creates cross-border transfer risk. OllaCloud removes that risk entirely.

Switching & processing companies

High-throughput transaction processing demands low-latency infrastructure. OllaCloud's Lagos-based infrastructure puts your processing engine inside Nigeria. cutting latency to Nigerian endpoints and eliminating international routing overhead.

Compliance frameworks

NDPA. CBN. PCI DSS. Three frameworks. One infrastructure.

Nigerian fintechs operate under overlapping regulatory obligations. OllaCloud's architecture is designed to support compliance with all three simultaneously not as an add-on, but as a consequence of how the infrastructure is built.

NDPA 2023

Nigeria Data Protection Act

The NDPA restricts the transfer of personal data outside Nigeria. Any customer data names, BVNs, transaction histories, contact details stored on overseas cloud infrastructure creates a cross-border transfer exposure under the Act.

✓All customer data physically hosted in Nigeria
✓No automatic cross-border replication
✓Data Processing Agreement available
✓AES-256 encryption at rest
CBN DIRECTIVE

Payment Data Localisation

CBN circular PSS/DIR/PUB/CIR/001/004 (June 2026) requires all licensed payment operators to store and manage payment transaction data on Nigerian servers. OllaCloud is built specifically to satisfy this requirement.

✓Transaction data stored on Nigerian servers
✓Aligns with CBN data protection laws requirement
✓Covers DMBs, MFBs, MMOs, PSPs & agents

PCI DSS

Payment Card Industry Standards

PCI DSS requires a secure network environment for cardholder data. OllaCloud's 3-layer firewall, compute enclave isolation, and AES-256 encryption provide the technical controls that underpin a PCI DSS-compliant card data environment.

✓3-layer firewall for network segmentation
✓Compute enclave isolation per workload
✓AES-256 encryption at rest & TLS in transit
✓Full access audit logging
