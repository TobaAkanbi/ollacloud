Who migrates to OllaCloud

Three starting points. One destination.

Whether you're running on-premises legacy systems, overseas public cloud, or a mix of both. OllaCloud's migration team has a proven path for each.

On-premises to OllaCloud

Moving from legacy physical servers or VMware-based on-premises infrastructure to OllaCloud's OCI-compatible private cloud. Eliminate hardware refresh cycles and VMware licensing costs while gaining full cloud-native capabilities.

The OllaCloud migration methodology

4 stages. Full testing at every step. Rollback always available.

Our migration methodology is designed to eliminate the two things that make cloud migrations fail: insufficient assessment upfront and insufficient testing during cutover.

01
ASSESSMENT

Workload audit & compliance mapping

We start by understanding exactly what you're running and what regulations apply to it. Our solutions team analyses your existing workloads compute, storage, databases, networking and classifies each by data sensitivity, compliance requirement, and migration complexity.

✓ Full workload inventory & dependency mapping
✓ NDPA & CBN data classification per workload
✓ OllaCloud configuration design & sizing
✓ Migration risk assessment & timeline
02
DEPLOYMENT

Secure VPN connection & environment configuration

A secure VPN is established between your existing environment and OllaCloud's Nigerian private cloud infrastructure. VCN configuration, security policies, and user access controls are set up on your dedicated OllaCloud environment before a single workload is moved.

✓ Secure VPN tunnel to OllaCloud infrastructure
✓ VCN, firewall & security policy configuration
✓ Identity & access management setup
✓ End-to-end connectivity validated before migration
03
MIGRATION

Phased workload migration with full rollback

Workloads move in phases, non-critical systems first, then core workloads. Each phase includes a full test period on OllaCloud before the original environment is decommissioned. Rollback is available at every stage there are no points of no return until you are satisfied.

✓ Non-critical workloads migrated first
✓ Full validation & UAT before each cutover
✓ Rollback plan tested before migration begins
✓ Zero-downtime cutover for critical systems
04
HANDOVER & MANAGED OPS

Team training, handover & 24/7 managed support

After migration your team is trained on OCI tools, the console, and operational runbooks. Olla Systems transitions to ongoing managed cloud services 24/7 monitoring, patching, backup verification, and regulatory documentation support. You are never left on your own post-migration.

✓ OCI tools & console training for your team
✓ 24/7 monitoring & incident response
✓ Oracle patching & performance optimisation
✓ NDPA & CBN audit documentation support
Why Nigerian organisations are migrating now

The cost of staying put is growing faster than the cost of moving.

For years, Nigerian organisations deferred cloud migration because it felt risky and expensive. That calculus has reversed. Staying on legacy on-premises infrastructure now means rising hardware maintenance costs, VMware licensing increases, and growing regulatory exposure under NDPA and CBN frameworks.

Staying on overseas cloud means continued forex exposure on dollar invoices, growing NDPA cross-border transfer risk, and the latency penalty of serving Nigerian users from European data centres. Neither path is getting cheaper or safer.

  • Eliminate VMware licensing costs
    OllaCloud's hypervisor is included, no separate VMware licence required. The saving alone often funds the migration.
  • End forex exposure on cloud bills
    OllaCloud is billed in Naira. Migrating from AWS or Azure removes the dollar exchange risk from your monthly IT budget permanently.
  • Satisfy NDPA and CBN in one move
    A single migration to OllaCloud closes your NDPA data residency gap and CBN compliance exposure simultaneously.
